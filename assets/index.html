Welcome to Pokebook

See your friends

In the Pokebook you can see all the friends already added to your account. You will find out about the name, phone number and favorite Pokémon of each of them. In addition it is possible to edit or remove them.

Add new friends

Our social media allows you to add as many friends as you want. To do this, just have the name, phone, image and most importantly: the main pokémon of your new friend.
